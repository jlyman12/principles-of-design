Similarity

"Our minds group things with similar properties, such as color or shape. “Like goes with like.” In this example, we group squares with squares and circles with circles. In layout, we can use similarity to create order and organization through unity" (p.76).

Aspects of a design that look similar will be grouped together in the mind of the viewer. Even though there may be 20 items in a design, the viewer may see a group of ten and two groups of five because the items have similarities in color, shape, or size.

Similarity can be seen everywhere in the world. People, items, and places that look alike or have similar characteristics are grouped together in people's minds.
