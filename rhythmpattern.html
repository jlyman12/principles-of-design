Rhythm/Pattern

"A pattern, whether regular or irregular, also may create a sort of movement we could call rhythm. Think of music, foot tapping, finger snapping, clapping and dancing. In graphic design, rhythmic movement has to with repeating items strategically â€” kind of like a backbeat"(p.53).

Your design shouldn't look like a bunch of random graphics and text thrown together. Everything has to work together to create a pattern that looks good in the final product. this can come from repeating graphics, words, or lists in order to add rhythm to the design.

Repeating symbols is common in any designs and in the real world. The couch in the picture has many repeating images that create a cool pattern to go with the atmosphere in the cabin it is in. Creating rhythm with repeating images and/or text makes for a very pleasing design in couches, print, or digital design.
